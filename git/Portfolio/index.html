About Me

Hello, I'm Sayantan Ghosh, also known as Lazy Coder. I'm a 20-year-old student hailing from India. I'm a self-taught enthusiast with a passion for web development and 3D animation. My journey into the world of technology and creativity has been a fascinating one, and I'm eager to share more about my interests and aspirations.


I am currently studying for a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Computer Science with Honors. My academic journey is a significant complement to my passion for technology, as it provides me with a solid foundation in computer science, programming, and other relevant skills.


As a self-taught web developer, I've delved into various web technologies, seeking to create interactive and visually engaging online experiences. One of my current focuses is p5.js, a JavaScript library that allows for creative coding and the development of visually stunning web applications. I enjoy exploring its capabilities to bring my ideas to life on the web.


In addition to web development, I have a keen interest in 3D animation. Blender, a powerful open-source 3D modeling and animation software, has captured my attention. I'm dedicated to honing my skills in Blender to create captivating 3D animations and visual effects.


My creative journey doesn't stop there. I also find myself drawn to graphic design and UI/UX design. These fields provide an exciting opportunity to combine aesthetics with functionality, and I aspire to craft user-friendly and visually appealing digital interfaces.


Beyond my academic commitments and creative interests, I immerse myself in the world of game development. Unity and Godot, both powerful game engines, are my playgrounds for creating immersive and entertaining games. Game development is not only a hobby but also a challenging and rewarding experience that fuels my passion for technology and storytelling.


I also have a special fondness for animation, particularly using p5.js, which is a JavaScript library known for its simplicity and expressiveness. It's a versatile tool that allows me to create unique and interactive animations, further showcasing my creative abilities.


In summary, my journey as a self-taught web developer, 3D animator, and designer is an ongoing adventure filled with exciting challenges and opportunities. I am dedicated to learning, exploring, and pushing the boundaries of my creativity while pursuing my Computer Science BSc Hons degree. I look forward to sharing my future projects and the knowledge I gain along the way with the world.
